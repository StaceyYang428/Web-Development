About Her

Stacey, a curious and creative graphic designer based in D.C. She loves transforming ideas into visually fun and meaningful designs, always adding a touch of humor and heart. When she’s not designing, you’ll find her exploring new coffee spots or hanging out with her pets. Let’s create something awesome with her!

Her Funfact

Stacey loves exploring new coffee spots around D.C., always on the hunt for her next favorite brew. Her creativity is fueled by caffeine and the company of her adorable pets, who love to hang out while she works on design projects. Whether it's discovering a new café or tackling a creative challenge, Stacey brings a fun and curious energy to everything she does.
